TRU_StoryBook

With the renovations of the stores, we thought, “Why not revamp another exciting spectacle of childhood, the Christmas toy catalog. We want to bring that magical feeling to the home as well.  By touching hot spots, the toys will upload into the parents Toys’R’Us account to easily share out to family and friends in one click. If a child continues to click certain items, that toy will move up in the Wish List queue.  - See more at: http://iamthereamblog.tumblr.com/post/69588143508#sthash.J1qcZqZe.dpuf
